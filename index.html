STORY

" My goal is to share my knowledge & experience with others.
"

About 15 years ago, I brought my son Alec B. Clawson to Los Angeles to learn the entertainment business. Since then, he has learned, developed and taught choreography and has worked with Jennifer Lopez, Justin Bieber, will.i.am and the Black Eyed Peas, and has credits on the Emmy and Billboard Music Awards and Disney's "Shake It Up" among others. He is currently dancing in Cirque du Soleil's "Michael Jackson: ONE" in Las Vegas, NV. He also continues to choreograph for Carnival Choreographer's Ball and mentor dancers new to the industry, especially through HOLLYWOOD BIZ.
